Bhopal Gas Tragedy - Remembering the Past

The Bhopal Gas Tragedy, one of the world's deadliest industrial disasters, occurred on December 3, 1984. It involved the release of toxic methyl isocyanate gas from a pesticide plant in Bhopal, India. Thousands lost their lives, and many more suffered long-term health effects.

Our hearts go out to the victims and their families as we remember this tragic event. The Bhopal Gas Tragedy serves as a stark reminder of the importance of safety and responsible chemical management in our industry.
